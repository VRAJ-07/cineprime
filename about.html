We love weddings! We feel they are an adventure to be experienced and captured in the form of timeless memories! Cine Prime was born out of passion to document love stories by Mitul Patel. Cine Prime is known for capturing REAL authentic moments in the most natural manner! Our company specializes in both wedding photography and filming with an artistic & personal touch. With our in-house dynamic team of skilled photographers, cinematographers and editors, we strike a perfect balance between stunning contemporary portraiture and photojournalistic storytelling.

We have a detail oriented approach and since we limit the number of weddings we take up, we are able to give enough time to each wedding for post-production. The team shoots in a manner that is unobtrusive and encapsulates moments, details, emotions (that you might have not witnessed in the general chaos) beautifully!
